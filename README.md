## Chocolate Sales Analysis (Pandas + Matplotlib)

Exploratory analysis of a Kaggle “Chocolate Sales” dataset to identify top markets, monthly seasonality, and best-selling products. The project includes a reproducible notebook and an executive one-pager with charts.

## Key Findings

- Top country: Australia led total sales; other countries follow with a descending trend.

- Seasonality: Strong monthly peaks in January and June; sharp dip in April (2022 view).

- Top product: Smooth Silky Salty ranked #1 by revenue among all products.

- Price vs. volume: Weak direct correlation between revenue and boxes sold—price alone doesn’t drive volume.
